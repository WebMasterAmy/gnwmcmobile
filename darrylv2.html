Great Northwest Music is a music family-friendly, family owned and operated music store in downtown Grants Pass. We offer our community a place where everyone from infant to grandparent can come and discover the joy of making music for a lifetime!

Great Northwest Music Academy offers music classes and lessons for all ages from babies to senior adults, including Kindermusic, piano, vocal, guitar, drumming, recorder, music appreciation and theory. Private lessons are offered for piano, voice, guitar, recorder and many band and string instruments.

Our local chapter of the national New Horizons program offers opportunities for adults who want to learn to play a musical instrument or return to playing an instrument learned years before.
